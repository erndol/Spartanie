About

We are a polish 20lvl guild in the MMO RPG game - Dungeons & Dragons Neverwinter.

The rules of our guild are based on cooperation, organization, sharing experiance and helping each of us to became a better player.

Thanks our rigid rules and well planing development by Guild Leaders we become stronger day by day.

If you are from Poland and you want to apply for membership just find one of our 7'th rank Members online in game or ask another member to get information when one of our leader is online.
